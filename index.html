William Schultz

I am a second-year PhD student in the formal methods group at Northeastern University where I am advised by Prof. Stavros Tripakis. My research interests are broadly focused on automated formal verification of distributed systems.

Previously I worked as a software engineer at MongoDB on their distributed database replication system. Before that I attended Cornell University, where I studied Math and Computer Science and graduated in 2016.
